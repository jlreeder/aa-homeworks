Ingredients

  • 3 beef marrow bones
  • 1 celery root
  • 1 onion
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 10 black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves

Instructions

  1. Roast the marrow bones in the oven.
    Don't be shy. Crank the oven to full broil and leave the bones in there until they're almost burnt. This should take between 1 hour and 90 minutes.
  2. Prepare the celery root, onions, and garlic.
    These can be roughly chopped in halves or quarters.
  3. Combine all ingredients in a crock pot, fill with water, and roast on high for a day.
  4. Take out the bones, place them on a cutting board, and remove any remaining marrow using a knife. Put the marrow aside.
  5. Strain the broth, keeping the liquid and removing all large solids.
  6. Return the marrow to the liquid, let the whole thing cool down, and store in the refrigerator or freezer.
